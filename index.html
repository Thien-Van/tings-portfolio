Projects

Raycast

Raycast is a skill-based 3D first person game with a minimalistic art style.The player starts on top of a ridge in an alien environment. It was a student project, made by a team of six within the third semester with about 300h of time put into it. My responsibilities were Game System Design, Level Design and Programming.

Neon Lines

Neon Lines is a fast pace flow driven action game. It is based on a rythmic concept of movement. It was a student project, made by a team of six within the third semester with about 300h of time put into it. My responsibilities were Producing, Game System Design and Level Design.

Shock Tactics

Shock Tactics is a single player turn-based sci-fi strategy game with tactical combat, exploration, squad management and base building. This game was made by Point Blank Games where I started as a Game Desing intern and ended up as a Junior Game Designer. It was published by Eurovideo Medien and released on 11th April 2017.
